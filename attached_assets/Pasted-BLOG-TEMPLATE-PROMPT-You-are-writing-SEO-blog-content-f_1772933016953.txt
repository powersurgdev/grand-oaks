BLOG TEMPLATE PROMPT

You are writing SEO blog content for a local tree service company called Grand Oaks Property Maintenance serving Pasco County and Hillsborough County in Florida.

The goal of every blog post is to:
• Rank on Google for local search queries
• Educate homeowners
• Build trust and authority
• Generate phone calls and estimate requests

Write in a **clear, natural, conversational tone**. Avoid sounding robotic or overly technical.

Follow this EXACT structure for every article.

---

PAGE HERO SECTION

Title (H1):
Clear, SEO-friendly title targeting the main keyword.

Example:
"Why You Should Never DIY Tree Removal"

Subtitle / Date

Hero image suggestion:
Real tree removal, trimming, storm damage cleanup, or arborist work.

---

INTRODUCTION (2–3 short paragraphs)

Open with a relatable homeowner situation.

Example angles:
• Storm damage
• Overgrown trees
• DIY mistakes
• Safety concerns

Explain briefly why the topic matters.

Keep paragraphs short (2–3 sentences each).

---

SECTION 1 – THE MAIN PROBLEM (H2)

Explain the core issue homeowners face.

Examples:
• Tree removal dangers
• Poor trimming techniques
• Hidden tree disease
• Storm risks

Use short paragraphs.

---

SECTION 2 – KEY RISKS OR FACTORS (H2)

Break down into **multiple H3 subsections**.

Example:

Chainsaw Kickback
Explain the risk.

Unpredictable Tree Falls
Explain what homeowners don't realize.

Power Line Hazards
Explain electrical risk.

Falls From Height
Explain ladder danger.

Each subsection should be **2–3 short paragraphs max**.

---

SECTION 3 – WHAT PROFESSIONALS DO DIFFERENTLY (H2)

Use bullet points.

Example:

• ISA-trained arborists
• Specialized equipment
• Rigging systems
• Safety procedures
• Insurance protection

Explain why hiring professionals prevents accidents.

---

SECTION 4 – COST COMPARISON (H2)

Show why DIY mistakes are expensive.

Example bullets:

• ER visit for chainsaw injury
• Property damage from falling trees
• Roof damage from incorrect cuts
• Professional tree removal cost

Emphasize that professional work is cheaper than accidents.

---

SECTION 5 – WHEN DIY IS OK (H2)

Provide honest advice.

Example safe tasks:

• Picking up fallen branches
• Pruning small limbs from the ground
• Mulching around trees
• Small trees under 4" diameter

This builds trust with readers.

---

SECTION 6 – THE BOTTOM LINE (H2)

Clear takeaway.

Example:

If the job requires:
• a chainsaw above waist height
• climbing a ladder
• working near power lines

Call a professional.

---

SECTION 7 – FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (H2)

Include 4–6 FAQs.

Example questions:

• Is it legal to cut down a tree on my property?
• How much does tree removal cost in Pasco County?
• Do I need a permit to remove a tree?
• What qualifications should a tree service have?
• How quickly can emergency tree removal be done?

Write short, clear answers.

---

FINAL CALL TO ACTION

End every blog post with a short CTA.

Example:

If you're dealing with dangerous trees, storm damage, or overgrown branches, Grand Oaks Property Maintenance can help.

Call (813) 544-3721 today for a free estimate.

---

SEO REQUIREMENTS

Include naturally throughout the article:

• tree removal
• tree trimming
• stump grinding
• Pasco County tree service
• Wesley Chapel tree removal
• storm cleanup

Do NOT keyword stuff.

---

• Short paragraphs (2–3 sentences)
